Instructions to use:

Important: This project is meant only for educational purposes and is not meant to provide actual financial intelligence. Investments must be made at your own discretion.

  • Enter space-separated tickers in the input field. E.g. MSFT NFLX
  • You may enter as many companies as you wish.
  • For some country specific companies, search the exact ticker from Yahoo! Finance first before entering. E.g. for Infosys Ltd, the NYSE ticker is 'INFY' while NSE ticker is 'INFY.NS'.
  • Since the algorithm is written from scratch in Python, results may take some time (maybe upto 1 min or more) to show up.

Notes about the project:

  • The tool uses statistical methods of mean and variance to calculate returns and risks associated with different portfolios.
  • This idea was first proposed by Harry Markowitz in his foundational work in the field of Quantitaive Finance.
  • Although not the best, this technique still holds historical relevance.
  • Thus the results may be far from being accurate. Read more about the project on the About page.
